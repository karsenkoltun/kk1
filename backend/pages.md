# Page Breakdowns

## Homepage (/)

The most important page. Cinematic, scroll-driven narrative.

### Section 1: Hero
- Full-screen, video background or high-end Kelowna photo
- Dark overlay for text readability
- NO stats or track records in hero — leads with action
- Three prominent CTA buttons: Sell Your Home (/sell), Buy a Home (/buy), Get Your Home Value (/home-value)
- Subtle tagline above buttons (personal, not corporate)

### Section 2: Rotating Text
- Option B recommended: Single centered headline with cycling word
- "Kelowna Real Estate, [Done Differently / Made Personal / Built on Trust / With Karsen Koltun]"
- Three CTA buttons below

### Section 3: Who Is Karsen Koltun
- Personal intro section with photo + short text hook
- Ends with "Read My Story" link to About page
- Dan Martell pattern: short, punchy, personal

### Section 4: Featured Listings
- Curated grid from IDX
- Card layout: hero image, address, price, beds/baths/sqft
- Hover reveals subtle animation/overlay
- "View All Listings" button → /search

### Section 5: Testimonials
- Carousel with large quotes, full names
- Subtle background change to break up page

### Section 6: Beyond Real Estate
- Two elegant cards side by side:
  - Kelowna Founders Club
  - Marketing with Karsen Koltun
- Small, clean, positioned as "more about Karsen" moment

### Section 7: Social & Content Bar
- Horizontal scrolling bar with social platforms + follower counts (like Dan Martell)
- Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, Blog
- Links open in new tabs

### Section 8: Newsletter / Footer CTA
- Email capture: "Stay updated on the Kelowna market"
- Feeds into GHL
- Footer: nav links, contact info, Royal LePage disclosures, social icons, legal links

---

## Sell Your Home (/sell)

Highest-value page. Must convert sellers.

- **Hero:** Rotating text "Sell your home [Faster / For More Money / With Confidence / With Care]"
- **Marketing Difference:** Visual cards/horizontal scroll of services (photography, videography, social media, paid ads, content strategy, staging)
- **Process Overview:** 6 steps — consultation, prep/pricing, media production, launch/marketing, showings/offers, negotiation/closing
- **Seller Testimonials:** Seller-specific, before/after metrics if available
- **CMA Form:** "Get a Free Marketing Plan for Your Home" — name, email, phone, address, timeline → GHL seller pipeline

---

## Buy a Home (/buy)

- **Hero:** Rotating text "Buy a home [In Kelowna / That Fits Your Budget / That Fits Your Criteria / At The Best Price]"
- **Content:** Buyer experience overview, link to IDX search
- **Neighborhood Spotlights:** Mission, Lower Mission, Glenmore, Upper Mission, Downtown — lifestyle descriptions + search links
- **Buyer Form:** Consultation booking → GHL buyer pipeline

---

## Free Home Valuation (/home-value)

Pure lead magnet page. Simple and conversion-focused.

- Headline + short paragraph + form + trust element
- Form: name, email, phone, address (optional: bedrooms, bathrooms, renovations)
- Feeds into GHL valuation pipeline
- Automated follow-up: confirmation email → personalized CMA in 24-48hrs → nurture sequence

---

## Search Listings (/search)

Full IDX/MLS property search embedded on site.

- Interactive map search
- Filters: price, beds, baths, property type, neighborhood, listing date
- Listing cards with hero photo, price, address, key details
- Saved search functionality
- Individual listing detail pages
- Every action feeds into GHL as buyer lead

---

## About Karsen (/about)

Dan Martell playbook. Personal, story-driven, first-person narrative.

- Headline: "The Short Version" or "Here's My Story"
- Scroll-driven narrative with photos
- Topics: growing up, entrepreneurial drive, marketing agency, real estate, Founders Club, UBCO
- Professional photo section → "What I'm Working On" section
- Links to real estate, marketing, Founders Club pages

### /about/founders-club
- Mission, past events, past speakers (Dan Martell), upcoming events, join/signup form

### /about/marketing
- Services overview, results, industries served, application form

---

## Let's Talk / Contact (/contact)

- Simple, conversion-focused
- Form: name, email, phone, dropdown (buying, selling, valuation, marketing, Founders Club, general, other)
- Dropdown routes to appropriate GHL pipeline
- Embedded GHL booking calendar
- Phone, email, office address

---

## Blog (/blog)

Long-term SEO play. Lives in hamburger menu.

- Content: market updates, neighborhood guides, buyer/seller education, lifestyle, marketing/business
- SEO: long-tail keywords, proper headings, meta descriptions, schema markup, FAQ sections
- Internal linking between blog posts and main pages

Standard legal pages.
