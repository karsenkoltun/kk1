# Navigation Structure

## Primary Navigation Bar

Sticky/fixed at top. Semi-transparent over hero, transitions to solid on scroll. Logo on the left (Karsen Koltun wordmark or monogram).

| Nav Item | Destination | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------|
| Home | / | Logo click returns here |
| Search Listings | /search | Full property search with map and filters (IDX) |
| List Your Home | /sell | Why list with Karsen, marketing approach, CMA form |
| About Karsen | /about | Dropdown: My Story, Kelowna Founders Club, Marketing |
| Let's Talk | /contact | CTA button style (highlighted, stands out) |
